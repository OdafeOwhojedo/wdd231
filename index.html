About Me

My name is Odafe Erikevwie. I am a Nigerian and hails from Okpe local government area of Delta state in the South-South geopolitical region of the country. I am happily married to my wife whose name is Edisemi Nancy Odafe and between us we have four boys and three girls. I am currently an online student of Brigham Young University Idaho via the BYU Pathway study program. I am studying Web and Computer Software Development and I hope to graduate with a degree in Software Development. In this first term of school year 2026, I am studying a course titled WDD 231 "web Frontend Development 1" and at the end of the term, if I successfully complete this term studies, I would obtain my first certificate toward obtaining a degree in Software Development. This course is highly tasking and it is focused on training us to be proficient in the design and development of the frontend of websites that have level of accessibility, interactive, performance and optimization using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I enjoy building clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces while continuously improving my JavaScript and CSS skills.
