Tips for Visiting

  • Research & Guides: For many sites, especially ancient ruins, hiring a local guide is essential for proper context, history, and access.
  • Permissions: Some locations, such as active palaces or sacred groves, may require permission from local authorities or community leaders.
  • Museums: Major city museums in Lagos, Benin, Jos, and Kano provide valuable background and house artifacts connected to many historical sites.
  • Preservation: Many historical structures are built with fragile materials such as mud or clay. Respect posted signs and support conservation efforts.
  • Best Time to Visit: Dry season (November–March) for easier access to waterfalls and parks.
